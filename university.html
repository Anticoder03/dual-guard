Why Universities are Prime Targets

Educational institutions manage vast amounts of sensitive data — from personal student information and financial aid records to cutting-edge research IP. Open campus networks and BYOD policies create a uniquely complex attack surface that requires specialized security strategies.

Security for Academia

Comprehensive protection for campuses, e-learning platforms, and research environments.

Campus Network Security

Secure Wi-Fi infrastructure, network segmentation, and intrusion detection across multi-campus environments. BYOD policy enforcement included.

Student Data Protection

Encrypt and secure student records, admission portals, and examination systems. Full compliance with data protection regulations including DPDP Act.

Research IP Security

Protect cutting-edge research, patents, and intellectual property from nation-state actors and advanced persistent threats (APTs).

E-Learning Platform Security

VAPT for LMS platforms (Moodle, Canvas), online exam systems, and video conferencing tools to prevent data leaks and cheating attacks.

Security Awareness Training
